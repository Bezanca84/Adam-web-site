The Microsoft AI toolkit

Three tools. Very different purposes.

M365 Copilot, Copilot Studio, and agents are often talked about interchangeably. They're not the same thing. Here's the short version.

M365 Copilot

AI embedded in your Microsoft 365 apps

Works inside Teams, Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Uses your organisation's data from Microsoft Graph. Requires a paid Copilot add-on license. It assists — it doesn't take actions.

Learn more →
Copilot Studio

Build your own AI assistants

A low-code platform for creating custom agents and chatbots. Part of Power Platform. Connects to your own data sources — SharePoint, websites, APIs. Deploy to Teams, web, or SharePoint. Can extend M365 Copilot.

Learn more →
Agents

AI that takes actions, not just answers

Agents have goals and tools — they can do things, not just respond. They can trigger flows, query databases, and run multi-step tasks. Built in Copilot Studio. Can run autonomously on a schedule or trigger.
